Gravity Blaster
A mobile-friendly HTML5 arcade survival shooter built with Canvas and procedural audio.
Pilot your ship through an endless field of debris, destroy asteroids, evade hostile UFOs, collect powerups, and survive as long as possible.

Gameplay
Gravity Blaster is a fast-paced arcade survival game inspired by classic asteroid shooters, designed primarily for touch devices and mobile browsers.
Your objective is simple:
Survive as long as possible
Destroy asteroids and UFO enemies
Collect powerups
Achieve the highest score
The longer you survive, the more chaotic the battlefield becomes.
Controls
Movement
Use the virtual joystick in the bottom-left corner to steer your ship.
Shooting
Tap anywhere on the screen to fire.
Bomb
💣 Clears nearby enemies and asteroids.
Shield
🛡 Absorbs incoming damage and destroys close asteroids.
Powerups
During gameplay you may encounter several powerups:
⭐ Multiplier – increases shot spread and firepower
❤️ Health – restores ship health
🧬 Extra Life – grants an additional life
💣 Bomb Pickup – adds another bomb charge
Enemies and Hazards
Asteroids
Primary obstacles that break into smaller fragments when destroyed.
UFOs
Hostile ships that appear periodically and fire at the player.
